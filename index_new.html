We Buy Gold And Silver!

For more than two decades, J. August Jewelry Consignment, located in Amherst, New Hampshire, has specialized in custom jewelry design, repair and restoration.

As New England's largest consignment jewelry store, we are experts in creating made-to-order redesigns of old jewelry pieces and new designs for unset stones. We also buy and sell antiques, estate diamonds and jewelry at below wholesale prices. J. August Jewelry Consignment offers the highest prices - paid in cash - for gold and sterling silver, which we use for our repair and restoration work.

Our jewelry boutique offers a spectrum of beautiful sapphires, emeralds, rubies, and other fine colored gemstones, as well as a wide variety of designer watches, wedding bands, pendants, estate jewelry, clocks and other antiques and collectibles. We offer unbeatable prices on Tiffany's, David Yurman, Rolex, Seiko and other upscale designer consignment pieces.

Not only do we buy and sell fine-quality jewelry pieces, we explain how you can buy and sell your jewelry safely - and intelligently. For example, we explain every option you have with regard to selling a particular jewelry item - whether to us or someone else.

Our company is sustained by a loyal client base, referrals and those who discover us in different ways. Our goal is to earn your confidence and trust and to ultimately become your "Personal Jeweler."

We believe that superior design and craftsmanship combined with affordable prices sets us apart from other jewelry stores and consignment boutiques. We invite you to visit our location in Amherst, to see our collection - or for a custom design or consignment consultation. For your convenience, we also make house calls.

If you have any questions about our selection or services - or if you are interested in selling your jewelry or hiring us for a custom design, repair or restoration job, please don't hesitate to contact us. Many of our customers never once step foot inside our store. We are at your service, whether near or far.
